Account and Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: June 2026

This page explains how users of the Nutrivo mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Nutrivo account and associated personal data, please send a request to:

Email: gunawanvincentius3005@gmail.com

Please include the following information:

  • The email address associated with your Nutrivo account
  • A clear statement requesting deletion of your Nutrivo account and personal data

Data That Will Be Deleted

After your request has been verified, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Account profile information
  • Food photos uploaded for nutrition analysis
  • Meal history and food tracking records
  • AI nutrition analysis history
  • Nutrition preferences and health goals

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance purposes, including:

  • Google Play Billing transaction records processed by Google
  • Security and audit logs
  • Information required to comply with applicable laws and regulations

Deletion Timeline

Verified deletion requests will be processed within 30 days. Backup copies may remain for a limited period before being permanently removed according to our backup retention policies.

Subscription Information

Deleting your Nutrivo account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription.

To cancel your subscription:

Google Play Store → Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions → Nutrivo
