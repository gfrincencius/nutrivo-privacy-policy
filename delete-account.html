Account and Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: June 2026

This page explains how users of the Nutrivo mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

If you want to delete your Nutrivo account and associated data, please send an email request to:

Email: support@nutrivo.app

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your registered email address or Google account email used in Nutrivo
  • A clear request to delete your Nutrivo account and associated data

Data That Will Be Deleted

After your request is verified, we will delete the following data:

  • Account profile information
  • Food photos uploaded for nutrition analysis
  • Meal history and food tracking records
  • AI nutrition analysis history
  • Nutrition preferences and health goals

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or billing purposes, including:

  • Google Play Billing transaction records handled by Google
  • Security logs
  • Records required to comply with legal obligations

Deletion Timeline

We will process verified deletion requests within 30 days. Some backup copies may remain for a limited period before being permanently deleted according to our backup retention process.

Canceling Subscriptions

Deleting your Nutrivo account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. You can manage or cancel your subscription through Google Play:

Google Play Store → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Nutrivo

Contact

For account deletion or privacy questions, contact:

Email: support@nutrivo.app
