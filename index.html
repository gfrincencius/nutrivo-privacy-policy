Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Nutrivo is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use the Nutrivo mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create or access an account, we may collect information such as your email address, profile information, and authentication data.

Food Images

Nutrivo allows users to upload or capture food photos for AI-powered food and nutrition analysis. These images may be processed to identify food items, estimate calories, and provide nutritional insights.

Nutrition and Health-Related Data

We may collect information that you provide in the app, including food records, meal history, nutrition preferences, weight goals, calorie targets, and other health-related goals.

Device and Usage Information

We may collect technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, diagnostic data, and usage activity to help improve app performance and reliability.

Subscription Information

Nutrivo may offer premium subscriptions through Google Play Billing. Payment processing is handled by Google. We do not collect or store your credit card number or complete payment details. We may receive subscription status information such as whether your subscription is active, expired, or canceled in order to manage premium access.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide food photo analysis and nutrition insights
  • Provide nutrition information and dietary insights
  • Track meal history, progress, and nutrition goals
  • Manage subscriptions and premium features
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or misuse
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Food Image Processing

Food images uploaded by users are used to provide nutrition analysis and improve the functionality of the app. Images are not sold to third parties. If images are stored for meal history or analysis records, they are retained only as long as needed to provide the service or as permitted by law.

4. Third-Party Services

Nutrivo may use third-party services to operate and improve the app. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Billing for subscription payments
  • Firebase Authentication for user login, if enabled
  • Firebase Analytics for usage analytics, if enabled
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting, if enabled

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers only when necessary to operate the app, process subscriptions, provide AI analysis, store user data, improve app performance, or comply with legal requirements.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no digital service can guarantee complete security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the app services, maintain your account, keep meal history, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also request the removal of stored food images or meal history where applicable.
